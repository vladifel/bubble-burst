Bubble Burst Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Bubble Burst (“the App”) is a Reddit Devvit game operated by Vladi Feldman (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how we use it, and your choices. By playing Bubble Burst on Reddit, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

When you use Bubble Burst, we may collect and store:

  • Reddit account identifiers — your Reddit user ID and username, provided by Reddit’s platform when you interact with the App.
  • Gameplay data — scores, lifetime bubble pops, combo records, leaderboard rankings, and related game statistics generated while you play.
  • Technical context — information required to run the App on Reddit’s Developer Platform (for example, subreddit context when the App is installed).

Audio and haptics preferences you set in the in-game settings are stored in your browser session only and are not sent to our servers.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide gameplay, scoring, stats, and leaderboard features
  • Maintain your progress and personal bests across sessions
  • Operate, secure, and improve the App
  • Comply with applicable law and Reddit platform requirements

How Information Is Stored

Game data is stored in Redis on Reddit’s Developer Platform infrastructure. We do not sell your personal information. We do not share Reddit user data with third parties except as required by law or as necessary to operate the App through Reddit’s services.

Reddit Platform Data

Your use of Bubble Burst is also subject to Reddit’s User Agreement and Reddit’s Privacy Policy. If there is a conflict between this policy and Reddit’s policies, Reddit’s policies control with respect to Reddit platform data.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain gameplay data for as long as needed to provide the App and as permitted by Reddit’s platform. To request deletion of your Bubble Burst game data, contact us at vladi.fel@gmail.com from the email associated with your Reddit account, including your Reddit username. We will process reasonable requests in accordance with applicable law and platform rules.

Children

Bubble Burst is intended for general audiences on Reddit and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
